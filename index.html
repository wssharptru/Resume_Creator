Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ResumeCreator

Can I use ResumeCreator for free? ‚ñº
You can write a resume, preview it, and save it in TXT or PDF format for free.
What does a resume builder do? ‚ñº
A resume creator is an online tool that helps you quickly create a professional resume from scratch or upload an existing one. You get access to customizable templates designed for different industries and job titles.
Are resumes made with ResumeCreator ATS-friendly? ‚ñº
Yes! All resumes created with ResumeCreator are optimized for Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS). This is important because many hiring managers use ATS tools to scan incoming resumes and select qualified candidates.
Should I make a different resume for every job application? ‚ñº
Yes, tailoring your resume for each job is important. Customizing it to match specific job keywords helps you pass through ATS systems and catches the employer's eye, increasing your chances of landing an interview.
Should I include a cover letter with my resume? ‚ñº
Absolutely! Even if not required, cover letters are important and show you're serious about the position. They're your opportunity to highlight skills that didn't fit in your resume and help you stand out from other applicants.
Can I contact ResumeCreator if I need help? ‚ñº
Yes! Our support team is available seven days a week during business hours. You can reach us by email, phone, or live chat. We're here to help answer any questions about your account or resume.
